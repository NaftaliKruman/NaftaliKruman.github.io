Reading

Reading has always been my favorite escape from everyday life. I am particularly drawn to fantasy, science fiction, and the classics for their unique perspectives on the human experience. My favorite nonfiction genres - history, economics, and finance - help deepen my understanding of the world. I track my reading on StoryGraph, so feel free to follow my journey there!
