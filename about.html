My Story

I grew up in New Jersey as a middle child. In a household characterized by spirited debates, I learned to balance different personalities and always consider other perspectives. Our home was filled with books and fostered a desire to always know more.


As a teen, I explored my curiosity through my studies and was encouraged to think broadly about my role within my community. After participating in a hurricane relief mission, I felt inspired to use my talents to help others. However, decision-making remained a challenge for me, and I struggled with committing to both mundane choices and major life decisions.


Thankfully, I enrolled at Brandeis University and took an economics course on a whim. There, I encountered a radically different approach to thinking that considers every nuance and its potential consequences. I fell in love with economics because of its rigorous approach to decision-making and its ability to drive social impact.


Now that I've graduated, I bring my passion for economics and social impact into my work. I use my analytical skills to drive meaningful change, no matter how big or small. Connect with me on LinkedIn to chat about economics, my work, or how I can help!
